All About Me

My name is Alex Day. I have a B.S. in computer science from Clarion University and I'm a currently attending Clemson University for a Ph.D. in computer science with a focus on path-finding. In the past I have involved myself in Natural Language Processing research at Clarion and JGMS Inc. and Autonomous Vehicle research at the University of Arizona. In my free time I enjoy grappling, reading, and long walks on the beach. If you want a more formal introduction you can check out my CV or resume. This website will hopefully serve as a place to keep my ramblings and accomplishments.
