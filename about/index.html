All About Me

My name is Alex Day. I have a B.S. in computer science from Clarion University and I'm a currently attending Clemson University for a Ph.D. in computer science with a focus on path-finding. In the past I have involved myself in Natural Language Processing research at Clarion and JGMS Inc. and Autonomous Vehicle research at the University of Arizona. In my free time I enjoy grappling and long walks on the beach. If you want a more formal introduction you can check out my CV or if you want to see some of the papers I've worked on you can see my Google Scholar. This website will hopefully serve as a place to keep my ramblings and accomplishments.
