Emoji Summarization

2019-12-13

For my senior year capstone project at Clarion University I worked with another student to try and summarize sentences by producing a string of emojis. We accomplished this goal through the use of vector embeddings. Both the initial and final algorithms are explained in blog posts, as well as a novel sentence segmentation algorithm that was developed as a consequence of the project. We presented this project at the Penn York conference(slides, abstract) in Clarion and published a paper at the 2020 PACISE conference.
